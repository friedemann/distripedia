Acanalonia is a genus of planthopper (Fulgoromorpha) in the Acanaloniidae family. A multitude of species have been characterized under this genus. They are distributed within America.[1]

Species

There are currently 58 species that fall under this genus.[1][2] Many of the species have numerous synonyms which will be listed alongside their currently accepted name.
