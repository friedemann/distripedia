Aaata is a genus of beetles belonging to the Buprestidae family.[2][3][4][5]

Species

There is only one species in this genus. It is found in Baluchistan and is one of the largest species of the Buprestidae family, reaching up to 7 cm in length.[6]

  • Aaata finchi (Synonym: Julodis finchi Waterhouse, 1884)

Other species

The genus name Aaata has also been described as a synonym to two species in the Microcionidae family of sea sponges (Demospongiae). The two species being:

  • Aaata brepha (de Laubenfels, 1930)[7][8][9][10][11][12][13]; also known as Anaata brepha[14][15][16]
  • Aaata spongigartina (de Laubenfels, 1930)[17][18][19][20]; also known as Anaata spongigartina[21][22]

The above are synonyms for Clathria brepha and Clathria spongigartina respectively (de Laubenfels, 1930).[23][24][25] The two later names are now the currently accepted definitions for the species.[26][27]
