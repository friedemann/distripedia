Tone Damli Aaberge (born 12 April 1988 in Sogndal, Norway) is a Norwegian singer. She became famous for participating as a young contestant in the Norwegian version of the Idol series.[1]

She was the runner-up in the Norwegian version of Idol in 2005, following Jorun Stiansen in the final. She has released three albums, Bliss, Sweet Fever and I Know.[1] Bliss and I Know has been certified gold in Norway.

She was one of the contestants in the Norwegian finales for the Eurovision Song Contest 2009, with the song Butterflies, and ended up in the runner-up position yet again, this time following Alexander Rybak. She also finished third in the Norwegian version (second season) of Dancing with the Stars in 2006.[1]
