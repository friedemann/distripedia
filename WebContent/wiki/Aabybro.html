Aabybro
Aabybro (Denmark)

Aabybro is a town in Jammerbugt municipality in Denmark. In 1953, its population was 1,188; by 1960 it had risen to 1,346 and by 2010 to 5,295[1].

Aabybro was formerly the capital of Aabybro Municipality in former Hj?rring County, North Jutland County, but by 2007 became the headquarters of Jammerbugt Municipality.
